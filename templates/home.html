The Twilight Saga is a both a movie and book series written by Stephanie Meyer in 2003. The book/movie tells the story of a young girl -Bella Swan- who moves with her father in small town in Washington State named Forks. she attends a new school and meets new friends. However, she meets a guy who is different in everything, how he looks, the way he dresses. She then learns that he (Edward) is a vampire. They end up falling in love, and by the time they both become aware of the danger they're in. Edward tries his best to protect Bella from anything, even her own self. One of Bella's best friends, Jacob, falls in love with her, but there's a problem! He's a werewolf.
Bella ends up marrying Edward and having a half vampire half human child.
There are four books and five movies. The movies had won so many awards and four of them were movies of the year.
